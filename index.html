We've got what you need!

Do you want to know who's checking in? Incidents such as fraud, theft, non-payment, misconduct, violence and disturbing the peaceful occupation of other guests during their stay with you, can be damaging to your business.

Be Alerted! All too often, guests go by undetected having left establishments prior to dealing with minor to major costs of repair to damage caused by bad behavior during their stay.

Nostay is easy to use. Simply enter the ID /Passport number of your guest when making a reservation or checking them in. A few seconds is all it takes to conduct a quick check to ensure your new guest doesn't have a reputation. Should your guest have been reported on Nostay, the details of the incident/s will be displayed. You are then in a position to make an informed decision whether to allow the guest to stay or not. Depending on the nature and severity of the incident, you can determine the best course of action to take.

At Your Service

Registration

In order for you to make use of Nostay you need to create a profile for your organisation by simply clicking the Register button. Here you will supply us with all the relevant information about your company and specify a secured username and password for accessing the platform on any device anywhere in the world.

Payment Processing

Once you've created your profile you will be redirected to our payment gateway in order to pay for your monthly subscription. For only R149,00 per month you are able to enjoy all the benefits Nostay has to offer to ensure that your customers and your establishment's safety are being put first. This includes notifications of changes in previously searched details and integrations options in your existing booking management software.

Profile Identification

Simply click on the validate button and enter the passport / ID number into the textbox in order to verify the guest. Once a real-time search was performed the results will reflect immediately in order to either warn you or provide you with peace of mind prior to allowing access to your premises.
