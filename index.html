We've got what you need!

Do you want to know who's checking in? Incidents such as fraud, theft, non-payment, misconduct, violence and disturbing the peaceful occupation of other guests during their stay with you, can be damaging to your business.

Be Alerted! All too often, guests go by undetected having left establishments prior to dealing with minor to major costs of repair to damage caused by bad behavior during their stay.

Nostay is easy to use. Simply enter the ID /Passport number of your guest when making a reservation or checking them in. A few seconds is all it takes to conduct a quick check to ensure your new guest doesn't have a reputation. Should your guest have been reported on Nostay, the details of the incident/s will be displayed. You are then in a position to make an informed decision whether to allow the guest to stay or not. Depending on the nature and severity of the incident, you can determine the best course of action to take.
